SingularTimeZones

About us

In our day-to-day we need to make conference and videoconference calls with different cities around the world. We could not find any useful tool for that. @thomasfuchs' everytimezone.com is the best one we found.

Besides, it was not very useful for us since we wanted to be able to change cities. I don't necessarily know which cities share a time zone. Also, we needed an APP.

Based on Thomas concept, and within the University internship framework with Alex Unger, we decided to launch the free app SingularTimeZone for the community and check if it is helpful in their daily work.

I hope you like it.

Thanks Thomas for your vision and Alex for your hard work during your internship.
